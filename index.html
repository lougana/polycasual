About Us

Brandster, Inc. helps people save money on consumer products within the Home and Outdoor Furnishings, Accessories and Décor markets. Working with established partners, we work hard to bring consumers brand name products at lowest possible prices. Over the years we have built our company creditability from ethics and morals that continue to be the foundation of which we operate.

Business to Business

Brandster B2B services and “cashback” incentive program help companies get exactly what they need, when they need it!

Brand Brokers

Unmatched shopping experiences thanks to our Brand Brokers. Get professional advice on brand, price, and value on today’s products.

Sales Channels

In addition to Brandster proprietary sites, we work with the top retail sales channels on the internet today to maximize brand exposure.
